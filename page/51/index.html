spotlights in three.js

Lights, camera, action! In this post will will be covering all three of those things in three.js, but with an emphases on spotlights. Spotlights as the name suggests is a directional light that will concentrate light in a cone like shape at a given target. This kind of light source differs from other options that will just brighten things up in general, or give a cylinder like beam of light in a given direction. In addition to adding directional light to a project, spotlights can be used to generate shadows, if the render used can do so, and is set up to render shadows.
